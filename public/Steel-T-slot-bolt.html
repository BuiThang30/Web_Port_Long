Steel T-slot bolt

A steel T-slot bolt is a high-strength fastener used with T-slotted tables, fixtures, and workbenches for secure, adjustable clamping.

  • Material: Carbon or alloy steel, heat-treated and zinc-plated or black-oxide coated for durability and corrosion resistance.
  • Head: T-shaped to fit standard T-slot grooves (M8–M12, etc.), preventing rotation during tightening.
  • Thread: Metric or Unified coarse threads for strength and easy fastening.
  • Function: Converts tightening torque into strong clamping force for holding workpieces, jigs, or tools.
  • Applications: Common in milling, CNC, and drilling machines for mounting fixtures and accessories.
