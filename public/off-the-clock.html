Maracas Single handed drum
Leave trumpet Flute - in progress

Description: I use a small plastic stopper to adjust the place of the holes and add marks. Each hole should be 2.5 cm from each other and the first top hold is 7.5 cm from the second one. Also there is a hole at the bottom and it should be 2.5 - 3 cm from the other end of the flute

One stopper is added is one end of the flute to make the sound.

Use a drill to make the holes that I have already marked

Just use the drill to make small holes and use a sharp knife to carve slowly to make the holes larger
