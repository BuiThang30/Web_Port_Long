15W KII Series Motor

The 15W KII Series is a compact, high-torque AC induction motor from Oriental Motor, ideal for automation and general industrial use.

  • Frame: Die-cast aluminum for durability and cooling.
  • Shaft: Hardened steel with precision finish.
  • Bearings: Dual ball bearings for smooth, long-lasting operation.
  • Cooling: TEFC or ventilated housing for quiet performance.
  • Mounting: Standard flange/foot mounts for easy installation.

Specs: 15W | 100/115V / 200/230V | 1350/1550 rpm | Stable torque

Compact and easily paired with GFV/GFS gearheads, it ensures reliable power transmission in tight spaces.

16mm eyestrap block

A 16 mm eyestrap block is a compact pulley used in small-boat rigging and light mechanical systems to guide or redirect ropes under tension.

  • Sheave: 16 mm grooved wheel on a stainless-steel or bronze axle with bushings or bearings for smooth rotation.
  • Side Plates: Stainless steel or reinforced polymer cheeks for strength and corrosion resistance.
  • Eyestrap: U-shaped stainless-steel strap for secure mounting to decks or control lines.
  • Axle & Fasteners: Hold the sheave in place for reliable load handling.

Use: Ideal for light-duty applications—control lines, travelers, or small lifting systems—where compact size and low friction are key.

IE3 Electric Motor

The IE3 Electric Motor is a premium-efficiency induction motor built to IEC 60034-30 standards for industrial and agricultural use.

  • Frame: Cast iron or aluminum for strength and cooling.
  • Core: Silicon steel stator with copper windings.
  • Rotor: Balanced squirrel-cage type.
  • Bearings: Grease-lubricated for smooth, quiet operation.
  • Cooling: TEFC system with fan and fins.

Efficiency: 85-95% with stable speed and low vibration.

Use: Ideal for pumps, fans, compressors, and conveyors.

Steel T-slot bolt

A steel T-slot bolt is a high-strength fastener used with T-slotted tables, fixtures, and workbenches for secure, adjustable clamping.

  • Material: Carbon or alloy steel, heat-treated and zinc-plated or black-oxide coated for durability and corrosion resistance.
  • Head: T-shaped to fit standard T-slot grooves (M8-M12, etc.), preventing rotation during tightening.
  • Thread: Metric or Unified coarse threads for strength and easy fastening.
  • Function: Converts tightening torque into strong clamping force for holding workpieces, jigs, or tools.
  • Applications: Common in milling, CNC, and drilling machines for mounting fixtures and accessories.
