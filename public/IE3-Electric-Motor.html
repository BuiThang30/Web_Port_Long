IE3 Electric Motor

The IE3 Electric Motor is a premium-efficiency induction motor built to IEC 60034-30 standards for industrial and agricultural use.

  • Frame: Cast iron or aluminum for strength and cooling.
  • Core: Silicon steel stator with copper windings.
  • Rotor: Balanced squirrel-cage type.
  • Bearings: Grease-lubricated for smooth, quiet operation.
  • Cooling: TEFC system with fan and fins.

Efficiency: 85–95% with stable speed and low vibration.

Use: Ideal for pumps, fans, compressors, and conveyors.
