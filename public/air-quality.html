“Long, simply an engineer”

The project is a solar-powered air pollution warning and purification system for urban intersections. It detects PM2.5 and PM10 levels, shows LED alerts (green-yellow-red), and automatically activates an air filter when pollution is high. The system includes sensors, a control unit, LED lights, multi-layer filters, a fan, and app connectivity for real-time air quality monitoring, helping raise awareness and reduce local pollution.
